NCC Rank and Insignia

Explore the various ranks and their responsibilities within the National Cadet Corps.

Insignia Rank Responsibility
Senior Under Officer - Responsible for maintaining discipline and leadership among cadets.
Under Officer - Assists in training and administration.
Company Sergeant Major - Ensures discipline and coordination within the company.
Company Quartermaster Sergeant - Responsible for logistics and supplies.
Sergeant - Assists in training and maintains discipline.
Corporal - Supports higher-ranking officers and ensures order.
Lance Corporal - Assists in basic tasks and training.
No Insignia Cadet - Learns and participates in NCC activities.
